Museum Explorer

Museum exhibitions and program carts are powerful media. We believe these agents provide first-person experiences that can lead people to make a greater connection with the world which surrounds them.

It is our hope at Museum Explorer that as collaborators with our clients, whom we recognize as our partners, we are together able to inspire thought, dialogue, and deep interest in all who visit.

Head:  Give people something to think about…THINK
Heart:  Give people something to care about…CARE
Hands:  Give people something to do…DO

Services

Exhibits

Museum Explorer creates interactive exhibits to delight visitors tailored to your needs and message. Our hope: to ignite excitement by providing thoughtful, hands-on tools and environments. We help visitors enjoy learning in ways that are self-paced, comfortable and just plain fun.

Learn More

Program Carts

Our program carts make your exhibits work harder by connecting directly with your visitors through informal learning programs. Carts deliver staff, objects and artifacts to your visitors wherever you want them. Everything is right there, ready to go, all in one place. Ready to take one for a spin?

Learn More

Program Materials

We help customize your exhibits by designing program materials to further develop visitor learning and participation. From gameboard pieces to art activities to slot machines – Museum Explorer will collaborate with you to provide that truly unique learning experience.

Learn More

Graphics

Museum Explorer’s Graphic Design Team will capture and project your institution’s messages and ideas to create the feel and experience you want to convey to your visitorship.

Learn More

Build

Our shop, led by our Vice President Matt Jaeger, is ready to build your design. Museum Explorer can make your vision for your exhibit a reality through customization, craftsmanship and care.
