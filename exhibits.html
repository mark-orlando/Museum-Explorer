Exhibits

We understand that great exhibits do not just happen by accident.

Whether your project is big or small, Museum Explorer has the know-how to set the stage for your visitors.

We know how to lay the groundwork for whatever project you have in mind. Museum Explorer can help shape your exhibit to your visitors, so that they can get comfortable, engage, have a good time and learn a little something new.

Frederik Meijer Gardens & Sculpture Park

For an exciting children’s exhibit at the Frederik Meijer Gardens & Sculpture Park, Museum Explorer designers Joe Faron and Dirk Urban created something wonderful.

The Curiosity Courtyard in the Children’s Gallery, inspired by The Secret Garden, encourages the youngest guests to grow their curiosity by play and engaging their imaginations.

In order to encourage this for visitors, Graphic Designer Dirk Urban created detailed floral artwork for the 30 foot by 50 foot carpet that underlies the exhibit, adding the garden elements needed to capture the senses and imaginations of visitors.
